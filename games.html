GameMaker Studio 2 (introduction)

Not gonna lie, GMS 2 is probably the best thing that I have decided to buy to date. I have got endless hours of enjoyment from this program, despite only having owned it for a short time.

Quite honestly, I took a 'break' from programming for quite a while before getting this software, I really didn't feel like I was getting much out of it other than making small things to show to friends occasionally. That all changed when I got GMS 2, a little over a month as of writing. Since then, I've produced over 30 little projects just because of how fun it is to program for me.

Surrounding this little info blurb you'll see some of my creations which were 'good enough' that I could bother to give them a HTML export. Have fun. (right now just slimeform but I swear I'll add more)

Slimeform Infinity 2

This is definitely my most playable and feature-rich game. It is also the first one I made in the engine. This game has random level generation, a functional level editor which can save and load from files, and pretty nice feeling movement if I do say so.

I wanted to put a HTML5 browser link here but GameMaker wasn't cooperating (html5 in gm sucks) so instead please download. Some time later on I intend to get it working, mainly it is just the level editor which isn't cooperating.
